# Defending Humans Against Attacks

Defending users against cyber attacks requires a combination of technology, training, and monitoring.

## Key Defensive Measures

### Anti-Phishing Solutions
Security tools that block phishing emails before users interact with them.

### Antivirus / EDR
Endpoint protection solutions that prevent or detect malicious activity executed by users.

### "Trust but Verify" Principle
Employees should verify suspicious requests, especially those appearing to come from executives or IT staff.

### Security Awareness Training
Training programs that teach users how to recognize phishing and social engineering attacks.
