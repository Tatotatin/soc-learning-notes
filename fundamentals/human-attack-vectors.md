# Humans as Attack Vectors

Humans are often the weakest link in security and are frequently targeted by attackers.

## Common Human-Based Attacks

### Phishing
Sending fraudulent emails with malicious links or fake websites to steal credentials.

### Malware
Fake or malicious applications executed by users.

### Deepfakes
Use of artificial intelligence to impersonate executives or trusted individuals.

### Impersonation
Attackers pretending to be legitimate users such as IT staff or managers.

### Other Attack Types
- USB drop campaigns
- Physical attacks
- Insider threats
- Fake job offers
